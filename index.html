《Simon》

Share music with the world

I've never doubted

what I've seen,

the highest mountains,

the warmest hands;

I used to live without a second thought,

wasting time boldly,

thinking I'd be immortal;

Only to realize,

when I finally opened my eyes,

seeing they had long been shut tight.


Simon, I just don't know,

where should I go?

Walking blindly, numbly;

Simon, I still don't get it,

why fate toys with me,

dropping me on this wrong planet?


A gambler who thought too highly of himself,

a desperate final bet,

well aware of no room for loss,

yet keeps betting;

Having nothing left,

yet I still glance back constantly,

waiting for

those things I lost, and those I'm about to lose;

Is it because I cry so often,

or they just can no longer feel sorrow;


Simon, I just don't know,

where should I go?

Walking blindly, numbly;

My Dear Simon,

I still don't understand,

When will we be able

to escape this planet,

can we?!


I always remember,

you said you'd always believe in me,

now I'm so sad,

afraid I can't find myself again!


I just don't know,

which way to go?

that I can say I have truly lived.

Simon, I still don't understand,

can we still prove our future

as we once hoped?
